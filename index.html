About Me

I'm a Driven Researcher with a passion for leveraging technology to tackle pressing Healthcare and Environmental challenges. With a proven track record in leading and contributing to impactful projects, I've spearheaded initiatives funded by prestigious organizations like the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India and the University of California, USA. My expertise spans across areas such as Data Mining, Machine Learning, and IoT Analytics.

Some of my Recent Projects

Soil Moisture Forecasting (SMF)


SMFS is an innovative technology utilizing advanced analytics and deep learning for precise soil moisture forecasts.
Its interactive interface permits users to tailor forecasts and explore scenarios.
SMF marks a notable advancement in soil moisture forecasting, promising enhanced decision-making across sectors.

Insect Pest Prediction

This project integrates presence and climatic background data from Envicloud and WorldClim to predict pest occurrence probabilities in the USA,
aiding agricultural stakeholders in crop protection.

Bridge Crack Detection

This project is related to automated crack detection system for enhancing bridge safety and longevity using the YOLO-v8.
It aims to overcome manual inspection limitations by tailoring models to task demands.

Automated Pig Detection Using YOLOv5

This project focuses on the development of an automated system for detecting pigs and analyzing their behavior to enhance monitoring and management practices. By utilizing the YOLOv5 model, this project aims to address the limitations of manual observation, providing a more efficient and accurate method for monitoring pig.
